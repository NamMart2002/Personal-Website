I'm the founder and current president of the Gainiacs club here at Texas A&M. We're a fitness club; our missions is to make fitness and nutrition education more accessible.

Often the largest challenges people getting into the world of fitness face stem from a lack of knowledge. Those looking to learn have to sift through hours upon hours of broscience, gatekeeping, and charlantry to find useful information.

By donating our time, me and a few of my friends are working to make the processes of healthy weight gain and weight loss easier for students here at Texas A&M and in the greater community.

While the club is still definitely a work in progress, you can use this link to join if you're interested. Check out our YouTube channel and our Instagram to learn more, or contact us at gainiacclub@gmail.com.
